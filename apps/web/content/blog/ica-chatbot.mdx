## Overview

ICA is **Analitica.id**'s AI-powered chatbot. My role was to collaborate with the AI engineering team to improve its response accuracy by designing a better knowledge management framework.

## The Problem

ICA could answer generic questions, but struggled with Analitica-specific queries — event schedules, pricing, course content, and partner details. The knowledge base was unstructured, leading to hallucinations and outdated responses.

## My Contribution

### Structured Knowledge Framework

I designed a taxonomy and schema for organizing Analitica's domain knowledge. Instead of dumping raw text into the knowledge base, we structured information into typed entities (events, courses, partners, FAQs) with relationships and metadata.

### Context-Aware Retrieval

By structuring the knowledge base, we enabled more precise retrieval. The chatbot could now distinguish between "What courses are available?" (catalog query) and "When is the next data science bootcamp?" (schedule query) and route to the appropriate knowledge subset.

### Continuous Improvement Pipeline

I established a feedback loop where failed or low-confidence responses were flagged, reviewed, and used to expand the knowledge base — turning user interactions into training data.

## Results

- Measurably improved response accuracy on domain-specific queries
- Reduced time-to-answer for common customer questions
- Created a scalable framework that grows with the business
