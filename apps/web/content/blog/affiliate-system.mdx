## Overview

A full-featured affiliate management platform built for **Analitica.id**, enabling the company to scale its affiliate program from a handful of partners to 50+ active affiliates generating over 200M IDR in tracked revenue.

## The Challenge

Analitica needed to scale their affiliate program but lacked tooling. Commission calculations were manual, performance was tracked in spreadsheets, and affiliates had no visibility into their own metrics.

## Solution Architecture

### Affiliate Dashboard

Each affiliate gets a personalized dashboard showing their referral links, conversion metrics, pending and paid commissions, and historical performance trends.

### Commission Engine

Automated commission calculation supporting multiple tiers and campaign-specific rates. The system handles edge cases like refunds, disputed transactions, and multi-touch attribution.

### Admin Controls

An internal admin panel for managing affiliates, approving payouts, adjusting commission rates, and generating financial reports for accounting.

## Impact

- **50+ active affiliates** onboarded and self-managed
- **200M+ IDR** in tracked affiliate-driven revenue
- Reduced commission calculation time from hours to seconds
