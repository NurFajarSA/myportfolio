## Overview

GlugGlug is a hydration tracking app built during my time at **Apple Developer Academy @BINUS**. The goal was simple: help users drink enough water throughout the day without being annoying about it.

## The Challenge

Most water reminder apps feel spammy. They blast notifications at fixed intervals regardless of context. We wanted something smarter — notifications that respect the user's schedule and integrate with their existing health data.

## Technical Approach

### HealthKit Integration

We used HealthKit to both read and write hydration data. This means GlugGlug plays well with the rest of the Apple health ecosystem — data syncs across Apple Watch, iPhone, and any other HealthKit-compatible app.

### Smart Notifications

Instead of fixed-interval reminders, we implemented a scheduling system using `UserNotification` that adapts based on:

- Time of day
- Last recorded water intake
- User-defined quiet hours

### Local-First Architecture

All data stays on-device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no privacy concerns. Core Data handles persistence with a lightweight schema optimized for quick reads.
