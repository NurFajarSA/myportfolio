## Overview

An internal tool built for **Analitica.id** to manage the full lifecycle of events — from planning and speaker coordination to attendee tracking and post-event analytics.

## The Problem

Before this system, event coordination happened across spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and email threads. With 90+ events per year, things fell through the cracks constantly: double-booked speakers, missing materials, lost registrations.

## What I Built

### Event Lifecycle Management

A centralized dashboard where the team can create events, assign responsibilities, track progress through stages (planning → promotion → execution → review), and maintain a historical record.

### Real-Time Coordination

Each event has its own workspace with task assignments, deadline tracking, and status updates. Team members get notified about their responsibilities without drowning in unrelated noise.

### Analytics & Reporting

Post-event dashboards showing attendance rates, feedback scores, and trend analysis across events — helping the team make data-driven decisions about future programming.
